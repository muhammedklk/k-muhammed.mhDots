F.A.Q

Your Questions, Answered

What services do you provide?
I offer end-to-end product design, wireframing, high-fidelity prototyping in Figma, and custom front-end development using modern frameworks like React and Tailwind CSS.
How do we collaborate during a project?
We'll primarily communicate via email and Slack. I share interactive prototypes for feedback, and staging links for development so you're always in the loop.
Do you work with startups?
Absolutely! I specialize in helping early-stage startups establish their visual identity, build intuitive MVPs, and scale their user experience quickly.
How long does a typical project take?
It varies based on scope. A landing page usually takes 1-2 weeks, while a full web application can take several weeks or months. I provide a clear timeline before we start!
